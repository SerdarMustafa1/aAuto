About

If you have ever thought about exploring more of this diverse country - its history, people, culture, beautiful unspoilt nature and historical sites; then look no further than Arne Auto for your travel needs. Family-run Arne Auto OÜ has been offering transportation services since 1997.

Passengers have a selection of buses, small or large; to cater for all needs. For your safety and peace of mind, our vehicles are fully insured and well maintained with drivers that hold a great knowledge and experience with all road types within the Baltics, Scandinavia, Europe and Russia.

Our kindest regards Arne Auto OÜ
